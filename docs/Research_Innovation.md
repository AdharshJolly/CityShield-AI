# Research Contribution 1

## Unified Urban Hazard Detection

Problem:
Most civic monitoring systems deploy separate detectors for each hazard.

Innovation:
A single unified hazard detector capable of identifying multiple urban hazards from CCTV streams.

Expected Benefits:
- Reduced deployment complexity
- Faster inference
- Shared feature learning

# Research Contribution 2

## Fire Vulnerability Scoring

Problem:
Fire detection alone does not measure risk.

Innovation:
Combine:
- Fire Area
- Smoke Area
- People Count
- Building Density

to generate vulnerability scores.

Output:
- Mild
- Moderate
- Severe

# Research Contribution 6

## Public Safety Risk Index (PSRI)

Formula:

PSRI =
Severity × Priority Weight × Persistence × Confidence

Outputs:
- Low Risk
- Medium Risk
- High Risk
- Critical Risk
